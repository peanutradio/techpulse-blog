The rise of AI is fundamentally changing what we demand from a data platform. AI agents and copilots are becoming virtual analysts — issuing queries autonomously, at scale, and at speeds that expose every weakness in a slow or rigid platform. Your mission-critical warehouse must now deliver ultra-fast performance and rich analytics not just for human analysts, but for fleets of AI-powered agents that expect instant answers from trusted data.
