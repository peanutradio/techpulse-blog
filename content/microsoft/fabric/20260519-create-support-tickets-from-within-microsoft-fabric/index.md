title: Create support tickets from within Microsoft Fabric (Generally Available)
---

Author: Roy&nbsp;Rubinstein, Principal Lead TPM -&nbsp;Starting May 2026, eligible Microsoft Fabric and Power BI users can access a new ticket creation experience within Fabric. The ticket creation flow lives inside the Help Pane, right where you go for help. By June 2026, Microsoft plans to transition Fabric and Power BI ticket creation to this experience. During this transition, users who previously created tickets through the Power Platform Admin Center may be redirected to Fabric.
