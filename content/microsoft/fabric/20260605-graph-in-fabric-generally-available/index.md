title: Graph in Fabric (Generally Available)
---

Graph&nbsp;in Fabric&nbsp;provides&nbsp;relationship‑first&nbsp;data modeling natively into Fabric, so context is explicitly modeled rather than inferred. As AI systems become more capable, their primary limitation is no longer data access but understanding how data is connected;&nbsp;something traditional,&nbsp;table‑based&nbsp;analytics struggle to express.&nbsp;Graph enables organizations to model, visualize, and analyze complex relationships at scale, allowing deterministic reasoning over paths, dependencies, and cascading impact.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
